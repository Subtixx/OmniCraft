## Deleting Items

<div style="min-height: 200px;">
    <Image src={TrashCanGui} alt="Deleting Items" 
        style="width: 100%; max-width: 200px; float: right; margin: 0 0 1em 1em;" />

    To delete an item from your inventory, simply right-click on the trash can with the item in hand
    or right-click on the trash can to open the GUI and place the item in the input slot.
